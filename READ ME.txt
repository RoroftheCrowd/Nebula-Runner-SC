--INSTUCTIONS--

1. Once the file is extracted simply click on the folder "Nebula Runner Final Product"

2. Click on "Nebula Runner.exe" to start the game

3. You'll be greeted by a config menu leave the resolution as 800 X 600 and you can pick if you want it windowed or full screen by ticking the box

4. Once the game has loaded simply click on the play button and the game will start


--CONTROLS--

W - Up

A - Left

S - Down

D - Right

ESC - Restart

Left Mouse Button - Fire


--SCORING SYSTEM--

Asteriods - 10 Points

Enemy Ships - 20 Points
